Process

A structured development process from requirement gathering to testing.

SrS Logics keeps delivery clear and disciplined. Most projects are completed in roughly 4 to 6 weeks, depending on scope, with each phase visible to the client.

Typical duration 4 to 6 weeks
Before build starts Requirements, workflow, and system direction are understood clearly first.
After handover Free maintenance and support continue for 6 months.
Timeline

The usual project path.

Week 1

Requirement gathering

Understand the client’s workflow, required features, business goals, and current challenges.

Week 2

Planning and design

Define the software structure, database choice, data movement, and overall project flow.

Week 3 to 5

Development and API work

Build the software modules, APIs, business logic, and core user flow according to the agreed requirement.

Week 4 to 6

Testing and final review

Test the system, review the software with the client, fix refinements, and prepare for final handover.

Why this matters

A clear process reduces confusion and keeps delivery professional.

Premium service is not only about how the software looks. It is also about how clearly the client understands the path from requirement to delivery.

After delivery

Support does not stop at handover.

Once the software is delivered, SrS Logics continues with free maintenance and support for 6 months so the client can use the system with confidence.
