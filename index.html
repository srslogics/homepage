Frequently asked

Questions businesses usually ask before starting a custom software project.

What does SrS Logics build?

SrS Logics builds custom software systems, internal business platforms, dashboards, finance software, workflow tools, and business-specific data analysis systems.

Where does SrS Logics work?

SrS Logics is based in Nagpur, India, and works with businesses across India, the UAE, and remote-first engagements.

How long does delivery usually take?

Most projects move from requirement gathering to testing within roughly 4 to 6 weeks, depending on scope, workflow complexity, and review speed.

Is support included after launch?

Yes. Post-launch maintenance and support are generally included for 6 months after handover unless revised in writing for the engagement.

Why custom software instead of generic SaaS?

Custom software is better when the business has its own workflow, roles, data flow, reports, approvals, or operating logic that ready-made SaaS tools cannot match properly.
