Careers

We are open to people who can help build the company well.

SrS Logics is interested in working with capable people who can contribute to growth, communication, and market reach. We are especially interested in individuals who can think clearly, produce quality work, and show strong output instead of generic applications.

Hiring focus Selective review
What matters most Proof of work, judgment, originality, and ability to produce useful output.
How to apply Send your work, portfolio, samples, or real examples of what you can do to the email below.

How to reach us

Send your work, not your resume.

If you want to be considered, send actual work that shows how you think and what you can produce. This can include campaigns, writing samples, prompt samples, sales material, content pieces, ad ideas, portfolios, or any work that demonstrates quality.

  • Email your work to careerat@srslogics.com
  • Please do not send a resume as the main application
  • Show real output, not only claims about your skills
What stands out

Clear thinking, good judgment, and quality work will always matter more than formal presentation.

We are more interested in what you can produce than in how polished a resume looks. If your work is strong, thoughtful, and commercially useful, it will be taken seriously.
