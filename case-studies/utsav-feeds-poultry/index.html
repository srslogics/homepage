Business context

In poultry operations, the challenge is not only production. It is information movement between farms, field teams, and ownership.

Utsav Feed Industries needed software that could support two very different users: the owner side, where visibility and control matter, and the farmer side, where reporting has to stay simple enough for daily use. The system was structured around feed updates, health records, requests, uploads, document movement, cost and sales entry, and monitoring from the business side.

Why it matters

A regional poultry business using custom software to bring farmer reporting, operational records, and owner monitoring into a unified digital workflow.

What the system needed to change
  • Make farmer reporting usable The farmer-side flow needed to support daily entries, feed updates, health records, requests, and uploads without becoming difficult to operate.
  • Give ownership a clearer operating view The owner-side workspace needed to show farms, reports, files, finance movement, parties, stock, and priority items in a structured way.
  • Connect field activity with business control The system had to reduce the gap between what happens on the farm and what management can see, review, and act on.
How SrS Logics approached it
  • Requirement-specific structure The software was built around the client’s actual poultry integration workflow rather than borrowed from a generic farm-management package.
  • Role-aware product design The owner dashboard and farmer app were treated as connected but separate experiences, because both roles need different levels of detail.
  • On-site delivery mindset The system was shaped for operational usefulness in live conditions, with emphasis on clarity, reporting discipline, and day-to-day fit.
Observed result

Owner monitoring and farmer reporting now work through one on-site poultry operations system.
