SrS Logics — Case Study

Operational Data Rebuild

Poultry Business · ₹1 Cr+ per Quarter
A high-volume poultry operation processing thousands of daily records across finance, feed inventory, mortality, and production metrics.
• Fragmented Excel & CSV workflows
• Daily manual corrections every evening
• Inconsistent finance vs operations reports
• No reliable single source of truth
SrS Logics — What Changed

Fixing the System

Before touching dashboards
The focus was not reporting or visualization. The focus was rebuilding the underlying data system for correctness and scale.
• Centralized ingestion across finance & operations
• Validation and reconciliation at entry points
• Automated daily, weekly, and monthly rollups
• Unified operational + financial data layer
The goal was simple: make the data reliable before making it visible.
SrS Logics — Outcome

Measurable Impact

From system-level fixes
+24% Feed Conversion Ratio
+45% Litter Quality
0 Manual daily corrections
Unified Operations + finance decisions
The improvement came from fixing the system that generated the data — not from adding more dashboards.
