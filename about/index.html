About SrS Logics

A Nagpur-based software company focused on business-specific systems.

SrS Logics builds custom software for businesses that need a system shaped around the way they actually operate. The company works from Nagpur across internal platforms, workflow systems, finance-oriented tools, and client-specific data analysis solutions.

What the company does

Software is designed around the business requirement, not around a fixed product template.

SrS Logics works with businesses that need software aligned to their workflow, data handling pattern, operating pace, and internal priorities. Some engagements are operational platforms. Others are finance systems, workflow tools, management dashboards, or data analysis systems built specifically for the client environment.

Company standard

The objective is not just to build software. It is to build a system that reduces confusion, improves control, and remains usable in daily operations.

Office presence

SrS Logics operates from Nagpur, India, with remote coordination and client-specific delivery support.

The company is structured to work with local businesses as well as clients operating beyond the immediate region. Delivery may involve remote planning, digital review cycles, direct requirement sessions, and client-site coordination where relevant to the software engagement.

Local credibility

The company’s Nagpur work includes deployed systems, active development, and client references across multiple business categories.

Current work includes finance dashboards, venue operations, poultry operations, loyalty workflows, data analysis systems, and other business-specific software. This gives new clients a practical view of the kind of operating problems SrS Logics is already solving.

Nagpur market presence

Built for businesses that want software ownership, not another generic subscription.

SrS Logics serves businesses that need their own operating logic inside the software: custom roles, local workflows, finance handling, reporting formats, staff usage patterns, and owner-level visibility.
